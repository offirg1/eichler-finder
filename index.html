Find your exact plan number

  1. Check your garage. Eichler's crews often nailed a plywood board high on a stud wall near the front of the garage listing the lot number, plan number, and tract.
  2. Look up your lot on the original site plan. The UC Berkeley Environmental Design Archives publishes scanned site plans for 36 Eichler tracts — each lot is labeled with its model number. Browse the Virtual Collections Portal or the Oakland & Imada Eichler collection.
  3. Ask the community. Eichler Network forums are great at identifying models from photos.
